TASK SYSTEMS LTD - REFUND POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Thank you for shopping with Task Systems LTD. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not, we offer refunds under the following terms.

ELIGIBILITY
- Items must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
- Products must be unused, in their original packaging, and in the same condition as received.
- Software, downloadable products, and gift cards are non-refundable unless defective.
- Customized or personalized items are not eligible for return.

HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
1. Log in to your account on our website and navigate to "Order History".
2. Select the item(s) you wish to return and follow the instructions to generate a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.
3. Alternatively, contact our customer service at returns@tasksystems.com or call +1-800-555-0199 to request an RMA.
4. Pack the item securely in the original packaging, including all accessories and documentation. Write the RMA number clearly on the outside of the package.
5. Ship the package to the address provided by our customer service. You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error (e.g., wrong item shipped).

REFUND PROCESS
- Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item within 3 business days.
- If approved, we will process a refund to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
- You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is issued.
- Please note that it may take additional time for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or credit card issuer.

EXCHANGES
If you need a different item, please return the original for a refund and place a new order.

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery at support@tasksystems.com with photos and your order number. We will arrange a replacement or refund at no additional cost.
