TASK SYSTEMS LTD - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. ORDERING & PAYMENT
   Q: How can I place an order?
   A: Orders can be placed through our website www.tasksystems.com, by phone at +1-800-555-0199, or via email at sales@tasksystems.com.

   Q: What payment methods do you accept?
   A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers for corporate accounts.

   Q: Can I change or cancel my order after it's placed?
   A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. Please contact our customer service immediately.

2. SHIPPING & DELIVERY
   Q: What are your shipping options?
   A: We offer standard (3-5 business days), expedited (2 business days), and next-day delivery. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout.

   Q: Do you ship internationally?
   A: Yes, we ship to most countries. International shipping times and costs vary; duties and taxes may apply.

   Q: How can I track my order?
   A: Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can also track it in your account on our website.

3. RETURNS & REFUNDS
   Q: What is your return policy?
   A: We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused products in original packaging. See refund-policy.txt for details.

   Q: How long does a refund take?
   A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The credit may take additional time to appear on your statement.

4. PRODUCTS & SUPPORT
   Q: Do your products come with a warranty?
   A: Yes, all hardware products include a 1-year limited warranty. Software products have a 90-day support period.

   Q: How can I get technical support?
   A: Visit our support page at support.tasksystems.com, email support@tasksystems.com, or call +1-800-555-0123.

   Q: Where can I find product manuals?
   A: Manuals are available for download in the Support section of our website.

5. ACCOUNT & SECURITY
   Q: How do I create an account?
   A: Click "Sign Up" on our website and fill in the required information.

   Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?
   A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.

For any other questions, please contact us at info@tasksystems.com.
