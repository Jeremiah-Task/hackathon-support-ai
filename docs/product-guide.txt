TASK SYSTEMS LTD - PRODUCT GUIDE: TASKMASTER PRO SOFTWARE

Welcome to TaskMaster Pro, your all-in-one productivity solution. This guide will help you get started and make the most of its features.

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
- OS: Windows 10/11, macOS 12 or later, or Linux (Ubuntu 20.04+)
- RAM: 8 GB minimum (16 GB recommended)
- Storage: 500 MB free space
- Internet connection for activation and updates

INSTALLATION
1. Download the installer from our website after purchase.
2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
3. Launch TaskMaster Pro and enter your license key (sent via email).
4. Complete the initial setup wizard.

KEY FEATURES
- Task Management: Create, organize, and prioritize tasks with due dates and reminders.
- Project Dashboard: View all projects in a customizable dashboard with progress tracking.
- Collaboration Tools: Share tasks and projects with team members, assign roles, and comment.
- Calendar Integration: Sync with Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar.
- Reporting: Generate reports on productivity, time spent, and task completion.

QUICK START GUIDE
1. Create a Project: Click "New Project", name it, and set a deadline.
2. Add Tasks: Within a project, click "Add Task" and enter details. You can assign tasks to team members.
3. Set Reminders: For each task, set a reminder to get notified via email or in-app.
4. Invite Team: Go to project settings, click "Invite Members", and enter email addresses.
5. Track Progress: Use the dashboard to see completed tasks and pending items.

TIPS & TRICKS
- Use keyboard shortcuts: Ctrl+N (new task), Ctrl+S (save), Ctrl+F (search).
- Customize your view by filtering tasks by priority, due date, or assignee.
- Enable dark mode in Settings > Appearance.
- Regularly back up your data via Settings > Export Data.
