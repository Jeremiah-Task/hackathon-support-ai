TASK SYSTEMS LTD - TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

Here are solutions to common issues with TaskMaster Pro and other Task Systems products.

SOFTWARE ISSUES

1. Application won't start / crashes on launch
   - Ensure your system meets the minimum requirements.
   - Update your graphics drivers and OS.
   - Reinstall the application: first uninstall, then download the latest version from our website.
   - Check if antivirus software is blocking the app; add an exception if needed.

2. License key not accepted
   - Verify you entered the key correctly (no spaces, case-sensitive).
   - Ensure the key is for the correct product version.
   - If you purchased through a reseller, contact them for verification.
   - Contact support with your order number for assistance.

3. Sync errors with calendar
   - Check your internet connection.
   - Re-authenticate your calendar account in Settings > Integrations.
   - Ensure you have granted necessary permissions.

4. Task reminders not showing
   - Check that notifications are enabled in the app and your OS.
   - Verify that the task has a valid due date and reminder set.
   - Restart the application.

HARDWARE ISSUES (for Task Systems devices)

1. Device not powering on
   - Ensure the power cable is securely connected.
   - Try a different outlet.
   - If using a battery, check if it needs charging or replacement.

2. No display / blank screen
   - Check all video cable connections.
   - Test with a different monitor if possible.
   - Restart the device.

3. Unresponsive or frozen
   - Press and hold the power button for 10 seconds to force shutdown, then restart.
   - Check for overheating; ensure vents are clear.
   - Run a diagnostic if available.

GENERAL TIPS
- Always keep your software and firmware up to date.
- Back up important data regularly.
- For complex issues, contact our support team with detailed information: error messages, steps you've tried, and your system specs.
